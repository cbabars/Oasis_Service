Overview

Oasis with its acquisition of Ranal’s manufacturing engineering services has imbibed over 2 decades & 2 million hours of expertise in the manufacturing space. Oasis therefore is now equipped to offer End-to-End manufacturing engineering solutions to customers globally. The team uses diverse technologies and tools to cater to the requirements of a wide spectrum of domains like Automotive, Aerospace and Heavy Engineering.

The company is backed by decades of experience in technology areas such as Robotics, Off-line Programming, Digital Manufacturing, Process Planning and Project Management. This, clubbed with a proven business model which is fine-tuned while working with a varied range of clients has enabled Oasis to cater to its customers across America, Europe, Asia and Africa.

The array of Manufacturing Engineering Services offerings includes Measurement strategy & Dimensional management, Simultaneous Engineering including Process design, Tool & Equipment design, Plant layout & facility planning, Process & tool validation using various simulation tools, thus enabling the customers to plan, validate and optimize their production processes well ahead of start of production. Oasis’s manufacturing engineering expertise is drawn from its acquisition of Ranal which has been a specialized player in this space with decades of experience and mix of right skill sets.
