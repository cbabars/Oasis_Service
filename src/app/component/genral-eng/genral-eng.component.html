Overview

In today’s competitive world where the cost of the product, time to market and product quality can make or break businesses, industry leaders are striving to optimize their product costs, shorten the development time and build product quality into the core processes of PDP. Oasis helps OEMs and Tier 1s to meet these targets through a variety of focused approaches that cut across systems, domains and industries. Few of the Oasis offerings aligned to above mentioned customer aspirations are:

Knowledge Based Engineering (KBE):

Customizations/Templates/Macros to speed up the design process, manage iterations and reduce manual errors induced in the design process. Oasis has a competent team of KBE engineers to work with various tools such as NX & Catia.

Simulation:

Physical proto development and testing is last generation. More and more OEMs are heavily investing into Digital engineering and Simulation is a key to this process. Oasis can support OEMs in
a. Product verification – CAE/FEA for strength, durability, safety, CFD, multi-body dynamics, multi-disciplinary analysis
b. Assembly verification – Digital Mock Up (DMU) and assembly/service simulation, kinematic analysis
c. Manufacturing simulation – Manufacturing simulation, Robotic simulation, Plant simulation Ergonomic simulation, mold-flow analysis

Value Analysis Value Engineering:

Oasis can support through a structured approach, various VAVE initiatives of the customer to optimize weight, cost or part standardization. Oasis has a competent team of engineers with thorough product, excellent engineering skills and sound manufacturing background to evaluate VAVE options to deliver value to the customer.

Virtual Audit:

Oasis lets you eliminate building expensive prototypes by conducting Virtual Builds, Virtual Reviews and Landing Studies on the products. Besides validating designs & MBOMS, Oasis can also generate Work instructions & Visual assembly aids.
