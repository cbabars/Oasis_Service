Automotive industry is continuously challenged with pressure on investments & reduced time to market for introduction of new models / variants. On the one hand in the manufacturing space this leads to a greenfield project or upgrade existing production facilities for introduction of a new variant on an existing production line. On the other in the Product Engineering space it leads to new product lines or vehicle facelift projects. In both the scenarios Digital Manufacturing plays a pivotal role.

The evolving role of the product designers to combine art, science and technology has been facilitated by digital tools that now allow designers to visualize, communicate and produce tangible ideas in a way never thought of before. Oasis is able to do exactly this through the acquisition of Automotive Elements which has very rich experience and the resources to execute projects in the Automotive domains of Interiors & Exteriors. Oasis supports customers right from the Concept Design phase through to Product Launch. Validation of designs for requirements of manufacturing feasibility, regulatory compliances, durability, crash etc. is done concurrently using CAE tools in the various design phases. The final validated product complete in all aspects is delivered to the customer within the set timelines. Latest communication and program management tools are employed during this entire process.
